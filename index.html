Elimination Rounds

  1. Calm and Composed (Cool & Calm): Tests participants' ability to stay calm under pressure and provide accurate answers to thought-provoking questions in community medicine.
  2. Multimedia Mayhem (A-V Round): Teams answer questions based on videos, audio clips, or images related to public health campaigns and issues.
  3. Dream 11: Teams bet points based on their confidence in answering public health risk-related questions correctly.
  4. Health Roulette (Take Your Chance): Teams spin a virtual wheel to determine the category of the public health question they need to answer.

Final Rounds

  1. Epidemic Express (Bhaag Milkha Bhaag): Teams race against time to submit answers to a Google Form within a limited timeframe.
  2. Buzzer Round: A fast-paced round where teams buzz in to answer questions, testing quick thinking and reflexes.
  3. Rapid Fire Round: Teams answer a series of rapid-fire questions within a limited time, requiring quick thinking and a broad knowledge base.
